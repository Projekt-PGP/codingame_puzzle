The Goal

Help the Astronaut escape from foreign Planetary System. To do it, the Astronaut has to find the way to collect superpower from the star. But be careful! Your fuel may end... Additionally, on every planet the Astronaut has to fight with aliens. Find the way to the star - stay alive and try avoid fuel shortage!

  Rules

Your program receives characters.
Each round the program should print next vertex you want to go to. Choose vertices between 0 to n, where n is the number of verticals.
Each travel between two vertex consumes fuel. On every vertex you have to beat an alien. Your initial fuel and HP are given as an argument. Amount of fuel that your ship consumes between two vertex, and HP needed to defeat an alien, the player gets as a graph representation.

  Game Input

Input

Line 1: First information is N - number of vertices.

Line 2: Second argument is E - number of edges.

Line 3: Third parameter is W - weight of vertices from 0 to N.

Line 4: Fourth parameter is C - connection between two vertices and weight.

Line 5 and 6: Subsequently you get your HP and F - fuel.

Line 7: In the last line is exit - the target planet.

Output
Single integer which brings you closer to exit vertex.
Constraints
2 <= N <= 20
1 <= E <= 60
20 <= HP <= 200
20 <= F <= 500

Get me out of here!

You are trapped in other Planet System. Unfortunately, the movement between some planets has been blocked by asteroids and you have to use safe routes only. In order to escape, you have to use the energy from the star of the system.
