The Goal

Help the astronaut escape from foreign solar system. To do it you must find way to pick up superpower from star. But be careful, your fuel may end. Additionally, on every planet astronaut must fight with aliens. Find the way without decrease HP and fuel below 0!

  Rules

Your program receives characters.
In every round, program should print the next vertex, you want to go. Choose vertices between 0 to n, where n is the number of verticals.
Every travel between two planets consumes fuel. On every planet you must kill an alien. Your start fuel and HP are given as argument. A value of fuel you need, and HP need to defeat alien, player get in graph representation.

  Game Input

Input

Line 1: First information is N - number of vertices.

Line 2: Second argument is E - number of edges.

Line 3: Third information are W - weight of vertices from 0 to N.

Line 4: Fourth parametr are connect between two vertices and connect weight - C.

Line 5 and 6: The next you get your HP and F - fuel.

Line 7: In the last line is exit - the target room.

Output
Single intiger that will bring you closer to escape vertex.
Constraints
2 <= N <= 20
1 <= E <= 60
20 <= HP <= 200
20 <= F <= 500

  Example

Input:
4;5;4 3 2 5;1 2 3 1 3 4 0 1 1 0 3 4 1 3 3;20;20;1
Information:
Number of vertices: 4

Number of edges: 5

Vertices weight:
0: 4
1: 3
2: 2
3: 5

Edges weight:
1<->2: 3
1<->3: 4
0<->1: 1
0<->3: 4
1<->3: 3

HP: 20

Stamina: 20

End vertex: 1

Example solution:
0 1

Get me out of here!

You are trapped in an other solar system. Unfortunately, the movement between some planets has been blocked by asteroids and you only need to use safe routes. In order to escape you must use the energy from the sun of the system.
